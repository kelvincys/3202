Our Vision


To enable a global local economy. Supporting local business and fresh produce.

Our Value


1. Think global and act local - we value local produce and local businesses and we aim to see these thrive in the online market place.

2. Community and connection - we know the strength of a well knit community and aim to allow clear channels of communication between the consumer, supplier and producer.

3. Quality - we value quality products and quality service, which is why we work hard with businesses to showcase the best produce and delivery it efficiently and conveniently to your front door.

4. Education - we know knowledge is power and to be able to make the right choices for your family, business or household you need to know were your produce comes from and how it has been prepared. That is why we work tirelessly to ensure lifetime traceability on the products we showcase, so we can trace them from paddock to plate. We gain inspiration and education from our suppliers and producers which can enable the buyers to create the best recipes with the most suited ingredients.

Our Mission


We are on a mission to create a sustainable marketplace for our local fresh produce businesses. We have started with butchers and aim to expand so that locals can shop locally for all fresh produce.
